The Computer Science and Engineering Division was founded in 1989 . The number of students currently assigned to this category is 60. The main purpose of this section is to educate the computer science and engineering department on hardware and software.
There are five Lecturers and one Instructor working in this department. All staff members have excellent experience in teaching. The division will have the latest infrastructure and the latest computer software needed to educate students on the new curriculum. The section contains three separate computer labs. A seminar / workshop is organized by experts on various technical topics each semester. And providing vocational training in various institutions. Students who have done Diploma in the category are eligible for higher education CET Entrance Examination for BE courses and admission to reputed colleges. .
